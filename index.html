  2. To play the game slide the 'MODE' switch to the right from 'Piano' to 'Game' position.
    The 'MODE' switch will change the colour from gray to blue.
  3. In a 'Game'position the 'Start' button will change the colour from gray to faded green.

    The 'Level' button will change colour to golden yellow.
  4. To start the game click the 'Start' button. The 'Start' button will change colour to green.
    The 'Level' button will indicate beginning of the level one.
